The Work

Four years of sport,
pressure, and volume.

Shooting sport at this level means showing up to 6am carnivals, tracking fast-moving athletes across multiple disciplines in a single day, and delivering hundreds of edited images on a tight turnaround. It's where I learned to work quickly, consistently, and under real pressure.

🏊

Swimming Carnivals

IPSHA and IGSA swimming competitions — early morning, indoor pool lighting, fast action. One of the technically hardest environments to shoot in consistently.

🏉

Field Sports

Rugby, football, cricket, athletics — outdoor sport across Sydney's top private school grounds. Tracking action across a full field while managing changing light.

🎓

Formals & Graduations

School formals, graduation ceremonies, valedictory dinners — formal event photography where the standard is high and there are no second chances on key moments.

🚣

Rowing & Water Sports

Regatta coverage and water sport competitions — shooting from the bank, from bridges, sometimes from support boats. A different set of constraints entirely.

Due to student privacy and child safety considerations, images from school events are not publicly displayed. Request access to the private portfolio — I'm happy to share selected non-identifiable work, or discuss the scope of the work directly.
