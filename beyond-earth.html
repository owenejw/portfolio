Planetary Nebula

Helix Nebula

NGC 7293 · "Eye of God"

The closest planetary nebula to Earth — a dying star's last breath, expanding outward for thousands of years. Known as the "Eye of God" for obvious reasons. At 650 light-years, this is what our Sun will look like in about 5 billion years.

Emission Nebula · Star Nursery

Orion Nebula

M42 · NGC 1976

The nearest stellar nursery to Earth — a region of active star formation visible to the naked eye as the fuzzy middle "star" in Orion's sword. Through a telescope it blooms into gas and light. New solar systems are forming inside it right now.
