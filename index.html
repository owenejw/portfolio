Content Creator

Macquarie University Media · Sydney

Over 3 years of hands-on experience in marketing, event coverage, and promotional content with Macquarie University Media. Part of a team that genuinely worked well together, we produced everything from cinematic videos and academic storytelling to large-scale event coverage and content alongside the Pro Vice-Chancellor.

The standout piece was a video I researched, produced, filmed, and edited with Bita Ghanbari, a profile of Daniel Kish, known as "the real-life Batman." The first reel pulled +3.1M views, 83K likes, and 5.5K shares. The follow-up drove +9.9M views and +180K interactions. I also contributed to Open Day 2025, reaching +2.4M impressions and +240K reach.

Jun 2025, Present

Marketing & Communications Co-Manager

HerVillage Foundation · Sydney · Hybrid

HerVillage was my transition from managing by task execution to leadership. I manage the marketing and communications function in an emerging not-for-profit organisation. That means leading a small team of creatives but also personally handling each and every event as the director and photographer there, taking care that everything that needs to be done is indeed done.

That includes everything: social media posts and videos, newsletters, all kinds of letters from partners and invites to events, as well as all promotional materials from community initiatives to fundraising events and company collaborations.

At events I'm shooting and directing, briefing other photographers and videographers, making sure we walk away with what the organisation actually needs, not just what looks good in the moment. I also manage the photo archive and edit across formats.

Nov 2025, Present

Communications Specialist

Woolworths Group · Bella Vista · On-site · Content & Events

Everything converged here. I create and produce executive-level content for the CEO and senior leadership of one of Australia's largest companies, and I do it at a scale most communicators never get near. Photography, video, graphics, events, AV, scripting, editing, it's all part of the role, and most of the visual and creative output is mine.

Scale

Content reaches every Woolworths, BIG W, Metro and CFC store across Australia and New Zealand, delivered to 200,000+ team members through internal channels.

CEO & Executive Communications

I produce content for the CEO and senior leaders, photos, videos, graphics, for major announcements, campaigns, and moments of crisis. I've shot headshots for the CMO, CRO, CLO and dozens of other executives, plus content for their LinkedIn profiles and BIG W's LinkedIn page.

Video & Studio Production

I script, film, and edit, from fortnightly updates with the Managing Director of BIG W shot in-store and on location, to high-production content inside a purpose-built studio with LED walls and broadcast-grade remote cameras. I also produce collaborative content with external partners, managing the whole process from brief to delivery.

Live Events

I plan and run the photography and AV for large-scale internal events, some with 3,000+ people in the room and thousands more watching live across Australia and New Zealand. That means being across the technical setup, the run sheet, and the creative output all at once.
